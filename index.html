Frequently Asked Questions

  • How come you only stock Acuvue Moist lens?

    We’re currently in beta so are only stocking the most popular type of contact lenses. We love Acuvue Moist and wear them ourselves; we find that they’re super comfortable for daily wear and suit a wide variety of customers. But if there’s a specific product you normally wear, email us at jo@strandhk.club and we’ll try and source it for you! We’d love to get your feedback on which products to stock next.

  • How long does shipping take?

    About 3-4 days depending on stock availability and specific location in Hong Kong.

  • How does the monthly subscription work?

    Every month you'll get an email reminder 7 days before we bill you via Stripe. If you don't pause or cancel then a few days after billing we'll package your contacts with care and send it off with love. You don't have to lift a finger.

  • Can I pause or cancel my subscription anytime?

    Yes! We totally understand the need to be flexible, so you can stop & start your subscription at any time through Stripe. If for whatever reason you can't via Stripe we're always just an email away at jo@strandhk.club!

  • How do you handle payments?

    All of our payments are handled via Stripe. We don't store your credit card information anywhere and you can cancel or pause your subscription any time.

Have another question? Send us an email at jo@strandhk.club and we’ll be sure to get back to you!
